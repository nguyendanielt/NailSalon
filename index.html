A Little Bit About Us...

Founded in 2012 and located in Mississauga, Ontario, Lavish Nails has become one of the most successful nail salon businesses, with an average customer rating of 4.8/5 stars. Our team offers many services, low prices that you can't beat, and most important of all, excellent results and customer service, guaranteeing your money's worth!

Services That We Offer Include But Are Not Limited To:

Manicure

Starts with cutting, filing, and cleaning of your fingernails and cuticles. After, you will get to choose a color of your choice to paint your fingernails with (optional).

Pedicure

Starts with cutting, filing, cleaning, exfoliating, and hydration of your toenails and cuticles. After, you will get to choose a color of your choice to paint your toenails with (optional).

Waxing

The area to be waxed will first be cleaned and dried. Warm wax will then be applied to area along with a cloth strip to remove the hair.

Eyelash Extensions

You will first decide on your desired extension length. The eye area will then be cleaned and the extensions will be applied afterwards by one of our professionals.

Massage

You will be brought into a relaxing, quiet, and comfortable private room. After, one of our professional massage therapists will get in touch with you to begin the massage procedure.
